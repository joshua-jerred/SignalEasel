void signal_easel::afsk::Demodulator::baseBandToBitStream ( ProcessResults &  results)
privateinherited

Takes the base band signal and converts it into a bit stream if the signal is valid.

Parameters
results(out) results The results of the processing.

The sample clock counts up to 40 and then resets.

Symbols are 40 samples long. This clock is used to determine when to add a bit to the bit stream.

Used to detect the actual symbol boundary.
